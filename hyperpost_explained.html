HyperPost, explained

You make one thing — a poster, a gig, a 15-second video. HyperPost carries it to every network at once, dressed right for each one. You post once. It lands everywhere.

dead.dance sits in the middle. Your content flows out to every planet. Planet size ≈ reach.

🌹 The idea in one breath

Bands make music and art. They're not video editors or social-media managers — and they shouldn't have to be. So we do the carrying. One piece of original, human content goes in; HyperPost curates it for each network, credits you, and sends it out — while you get back to the music. We're not a tool you operate. We're a family that distributes your art.

Where your content goes — one at a time

f

Facebook

the home planet · two-way

Where the Dead family already lives. HyperPost posts your show and your poster video into the right groups and pages — and it works both ways: it also brings your Facebook friends and followers into DeadDance. This is the gravity well; everything else orbits it.

◎

Instagram

the visual feed

Where the poster and the 15-second video shine. Instagram won't let a caption hold a clickable link, so HyperPost puts your deaddance.app/g link in the first comment and your bio — the art fills the feed, the link still travels.

◐

Snapchat

quick + young

Fast, disappearing stories that reach a younger crowd the other networks miss. Good for day-of energy — "doors in an hour," the lot, the line.

G

Google

sign-in + being found

Two jobs. One-tap sign-in (no password to remember), and search — HyperPost makes your shows findable when someone Googles the band or the venue. Not a feed you post to; the front door people arrive through.

✉

SMTP — email

the channel you own

SMTP is just the plumbing email uses to send a message — think of it as the mail truck. HyperPost uses it to drop your show announcement and poster video straight into your fans' inboxes (only the ones who opted in). No algorithm decides who sees it — you own this list, and email is the most reliable reach there is. When Facebook changes the rules, your inbox line still works.

▶

YouTube

where the video lives

Home for the 15-second poster videos. It gives every clip a real link that plays inline everywhere and is searchable forever. We handle the upload for you (you never touch a YouTube account), and because the music is band-owned or cleared, it sails past YouTube's copyright checks.

✆

SMS — text

highest open rate, used gently

A text gets read within minutes — so we use it sparingly and only with opt-in: on-sale alerts, doors, a "can't make it? free your ticket to a Miracle" nudge. Never spam. The most personal line to the fan.

𝕏

X

real-time

The live wire — on-sales, setlist-in-progress, lot buzz, the moment-by-moment. Short, fast, now.

♪

TikTok

the discovery engine

Short video is how new fans find you. The poster videos and show clips live here to be discovered by people who've never heard the band — the top of the funnel that fills the room.

↺

Reddit

the deep fans

The most devoted heads gather here (r/gratefuldead and the like). Community-first — HyperPost shares, it doesn't sell. Come correct and this crowd carries you further than any ad ever could.

And it's all one motion

You approve a default we curate — or edit it in your own voice — and HyperPost handles the ten different sizes, formats, and rules for you. One yes. Every network. Your name on all of it. That's the whole machine: original human content, distributed with love.
