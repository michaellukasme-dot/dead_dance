Where the money comes from

Every band on DeadDance can pick your work

When a band posts a show, your catalog is right there. Two ways they buy:

🖼️

Pick your poster for their show

A band grabs one of your existing pieces as their show poster — printed, sold to their fans, you get paid per print.

✍️

Request an original

"Make us something for the Ardmore run." They brief you, you dream it up, you set the commission price. One-of-one or a whole print run.

On the backend this catalog is offered to every band on DeadDance the moment they build a show poster — you don't chase the work, the work finds you.
The deal

Why put your store here

💸 You set every price

Per poster, per print, per commission. We never touch your pricing.

🆓 Free to list

No fee to build your store or hold your catalog. The house takes a small share of a sale — only when you actually sell.

🎸 A built-in audience

Every band and every fan on the network — people who buy posters — see your work in context, at the show.

🖨️ Print-on-demand ready

We handle the print/ship plumbing (Printful / Custom Ink) so you ship art, not boxes — or fulfill it yourself.

✍️ Commissions on tap

"Open to originals" turns every show into a possible custom job. You dream it up.
