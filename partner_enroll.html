✅ How to take part — legally, inside Facebook's rules

Simple and clean. This is human, voluntary sharing — never automation or scraping.

DO — share your gems to the dead.dance page. When a post in your group is worth spreading, use Facebook's own Share button to share it to the dead.dance page. Human click, every time.
DO — keep attribution + links. Shares carry the original poster and a link back to Facebook. Credit stays with the creator.
DO — respect your group's rules and Facebook's terms. Only share what your group allows; honor members' privacy.
DON'T — no bots, no scraping, no auto-posting. Nothing automated touches Facebook. If it isn't a human choosing to share, we don't do it.
DON'T — no reposting private/members-only content publicly. When in doubt, ask the poster or leave it.
Disclosure: because Curators are compensated, your sharing is a rewarded partnership — we disclose that, per FTC guidance. It stays honest.
