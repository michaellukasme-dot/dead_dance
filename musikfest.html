What the locals know (and you don't yet)
  • 90% of Musikfest is free. Only the Wind Creek Steel Stage costs money — everything else is un-gated.
  • Don't walk north↔south. They're across the Lehigh River. Ride the free LANTA Gold Line shuttle; it stops at Stadtplatz (Payrow Plaza) and SteelStacks.
  • The jam & Dead-tribute acts land on Volksplatz (downtown, along the creek) and Americaplatz / Levitt Pavilion (SteelStacks). Watch those two.
  • Get the mug. The insulated Musikfest mug (2026 art by Doug Boehm) gets you a discount on every refill — and quietly says "I've done this before."
  • Food & beer run on 50¢ paper tickets, sold in blocks of 20 at every platz. Buy a strip first.
  • Rookie trap: Americaplatz (free Levitt lawn) ≠ Steel Stage (paid) — they're 150 ft apart at SteelStacks.
