📶 Event logistics — how a night runs works today

The whole flow hangs off two things the house already has: the calendar (venue-created events) and every user's profile QR. No new accounts, no passwords. The three gates are cleanly separated — identity vs. room/Wi-Fi vs. paid entry. Everything here is shippable now; only the licensed songbook waits.
Identity / sign-in

Your profile QR

Scan your existing QR (or you're already signed in). Score, hearts, Roses, queue slot & badges attach to the real you. Passwordless, instant.
Room + Wi-Fi access

Event QR + Wi-Fi QR

A standard Wi-Fi QR joins the phone to the room's network natively (no typing). The shareable Event QR drops you into the room — walk-up or RSVP. Venue can rotate/expire it per night.
Paid entry

The ticket gate

A charged event gates on the ticket (bought from the calendar), not just Wi-Fi — so a shared code can't dodge the paywall. Ticket = entry. Free backyard events skip this gate.
